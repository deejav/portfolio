E-commerce Growth · Marketing Lead · 6 Months

Scaling a D2C Skincare Brand from $40K to $180K/Month

Full-funnel growth strategy combining paid media, email automation, and CRO to triple monthly revenue while reducing CAC by 28%.

The Challenge

A direct-to-consumer skincare brand was spending $25K/month on Meta and Google Ads with diminishing returns. Email marketing was underutilized, the website had a 1.8% conversion rate, and customer retention was below industry average. The CEO needed someone to own the marketing function and build a scalable growth engine.

My Approach

01
Paid Media Strategy & Optimization

Audited all Meta and Google Ads campaigns. Restructured ad account with full-funnel campaign architecture — TOF (awareness via UGC creator content), MOF (retargeting with social proof), BOF (dynamic product ads with urgency). Implemented Advantage+ Shopping campaigns on Meta and Performance Max on Google. Launched TikTok Ads with creator partnerships targeting Gen Z and millennial audiences.

02
Email Marketing & CRM Automation

Migrated to Klaviyo and built a full lifecycle email program: Welcome series (3-email sequence, 48% open rate), Abandoned cart flow (12% recovery rate), Post-purchase nurture with cross-sell, Winback flow for lapsed customers (60+ days), and VIP segment with exclusive early access. Implemented SMS marketing for flash sales and launches, generating 22% of total email revenue.

03
Conversion Rate Optimization

Led systematic A/B testing program across the entire purchase funnel. Redesigned product pages with lifestyle imagery, social proof badges, and urgency elements. Optimized checkout flow — removed friction points, added trust signals, implemented shop pay. Tested landing page variations for paid traffic with dedicated offer pages per campaign.

04
Analytics, Reporting & Team Leadership

Built GA4 dashboards tracking full-funnel metrics from ad impression to repeat purchase. Set up attribution modeling to accurately assess channel performance. Presented weekly performance reports to CEO with actionable recommendations. Hired and mentored a junior marketing coordinator and managed relationships with 3 freelance creatives.

The Results

↑
Revenue tripled from $40K to $180K/month within 6 months, with organic and email now contributing 35% of total revenue.
↓
Customer acquisition cost dropped 28% through creative testing, funnel optimization, and audience refinement across Meta, Google, and TikTok.
↑
Customer retention rate increased 60% via Klaviyo lifecycle flows, loyalty segmentation, and personalized post-purchase journeys.
↑
Conversion rate more than doubled from 1.8% to 4.2% through systematic CRO — product page redesigns, checkout optimization, and landing page testing.
